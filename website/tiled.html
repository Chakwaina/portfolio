Tiled, 2012, Aluminum, tile, and wood. 3D design. I created this in highschool for an assignment. we were given a list of artists and were reqired to pick a piece that they did and recreate it with a unique spin. I chose David Smith a sculpture in the early 1900s. He died in 1965. he worked largley with steel and aluminum. I choose to recreate his piece CubiXII. Today the sculpture sits in the Hirshhorn Museum and Scuplture Garden.
